Weather

Discover Taniti's tropical climate with warm temperatures and refreshing ocean breezes all year round. Enjoy beach activities and outdoor adventures during summers and mild weather during winters. Taniti is a welcoming destination that has something to offer anytime you visit.

Getting Here

Taniti is a beautiful place that people from around the world like to visit. You can get there by air or by a small cruise ship that stops at Yellow Leaf Bay once a week. The airport can handle small jets and planes with propellers. We are working on making the airport bigger so that bigger planes can come to the island.

Culture

Experience the warm hospitality, vibrant traditions, colorful festivals, and authentic island cuisine of Tanitian culture. Taniti uses the U.S. dollar as its currency, with many businesses accepting euros and yen. Major credit cards are widely accepted, and many younger Tanitians speak fluent English.

Frequently Asked Questions

Taniti enjoys a warm tropical climate year-round, with balmy summers and mild winters. Perfect for outdoor activities and beach relaxation.
Taniti uses the U.S. dollar as its currency, with many businesses accepting euros and yen. Major credit cards are widely accepted.
While many younger Tanitians speak fluent English, especially in urban areas, you'll find less English spoken in rural areas, particularly by older residents.
Taniti is in the Pacific Time Zone (PT), which is 8 hours behind Coordinated Universal Time (UTC-8).
The standard voltage in Taniti is 120V, same as in the United States. The standard frequency is 60Hz.
You can't buy or serve alcohol from midnight until 9:00 a.m. The legal drinking age on Taniti is 18, but it's not enforced strictly.
