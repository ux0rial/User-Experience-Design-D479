About Taniti

Welcome to Taniti, a captivating tropical paradise nestled in the heart of the Pacific. Spanning less than 500 square miles, our island boasts a diverse landscape, from sandy and rocky beaches to lush rainforests and a mountainous interior crowned by a small, active volcano. With an indigenous population of around 20,000, Taniti's culture and economy have long been rooted in fishing and agriculture, although tourism is on the rise.

Tourist Amenities

  • Restaurants

    Indulge in Taniti's culinary delights at our 10 diverse restaurants. Whether you're craving local fish and rice, American-style meals, or Pan-Asian cuisine, there's something to satisfy every palate.

  • Grocery Stores

    Shop for essentials at our two supermarkets, two smaller grocery stores, or our 24-hour convenience store, ensuring you have everything you need for your stay.

  • Lodging

    Find your perfect retreat among Taniti's varied lodging options, from budget-friendly hostels to a luxurious four-star resort. Rest assured, all accommodations are rigorously inspected by the Tanitian government to ensure quality and comfort.

  • Entertainment

    Beyond our stunning natural attractions, Taniti offers a plethora of activities to keep you entertained. Explore local history at our museum, embark on fishing tours, snorkel in pristine waters, zip-line through the rainforest, or enjoy a night out at pubs, a microbrewery, and a dance club. For those seeking relaxation, a movie theater, arcade, art galleries, and bowling alleys await. Plus, get ready to tee off at our upcoming nine-hole golf course!

  • Sightseeing

    Most visitors gravitate towards Taniti City, renowned for its native architecture and breathtaking beaches that encircle Yellow Leaf Bay. Discover the island's beauty through boat or bus tours, rainforest hikes, or exhilarating visits to our active volcano.

  • Transportation

    Arriving in Taniti is a breeze with our small airport accommodating both small jets and propeller planes. We're also expanding our airport to welcome larger jets soon. Ground transportation within Taniti City is convenient with public buses running daily and taxis readily available. Rental cars and bicycles are also options for exploring our walkable city and surrounding areas, including the vibrant Merriton Landing on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay.

Experience the magic of Taniti, where adventure, culture, and relaxation await at every turn!
